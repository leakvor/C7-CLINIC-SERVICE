Primary Care Service

These are general medical services provided by healthcare professionals, such as doctors, nurses, and physician assistants. This can include routine check-ups, vaccinations, preventive care, and treatment for common illnesses and injuries.

Specialized Medical Services:

Private clinics may offer specialized medical services in various fields, such as dermatology, cardiology, orthopedics, gynecology, pediatrics, and more. These services are provided by doctors with expertise in their respective fields.

Diagnostic Services

Private clinics often offer diagnostic services, such as laboratory tests, imaging services (X-rays, ultrasounds, MRI scans), and other diagnostic procedures to help diagnose and monitor medical conditions.

Minor Surgical Procedures

Some private clinics may also offer minor surgical procedures that can be performed in an outpatient setting, such as mole removal, biopsies, suturing of minor wounds, and joint injections..

Wellness and Preventive Services

Private clinics may provide wellness and preventive services, such as health screenings, vaccinations, lifestyle counseling, and chronic disease management.
