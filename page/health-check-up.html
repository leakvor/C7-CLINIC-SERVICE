general health check-up

physical examination, medical history review, and basic laboratory tests to assess overall health and detect any potential health issues.

well woman check-up

includes a pelvic examination, breast examination, Pap smear, and other tests to screen for gynecological conditions and assess reproductive health.

well man check-up

specifically for men and may include prostate cancer screening, testicular examination, and other tests to assess male-specific health concerns.

cardiovascular health check

blood pressure measurement, cholesterol level assessment, electrocardiogram (ECG), and stress tests to evaluate heart health and identify any risk factors.

diabetes check-up

assessing blood sugar levels through fasting blood glucose tests or glycated hemoglobin (HbA1c) tests to diagnose and monitor diabetes.

cancer screening

tests based on age, gender, and individual risk factors. Examples include mammograms for breast cancer screening, colonoscopies for colorectal cancer screening,

bone health check up

This may involve bone density scans (DXA scans) to assess bone strength and screen for osteoporosis or other bone-related conditions.

immunization check-up
